Australian Indietronica Hitmaker Drops New Single. - Boo Seeka: Dream

Injects a luscious and futuristic vocal mix into the signature alchemy of a pulsating kick drum and sizzling live energy.
CHILLFILTR - Dec 13, 2021

After bursting onto the stage in 2015 with breakthrough debut single "Kingdom Leader," Boo Seeka has remained a staple of the Australian music scene where they continue to bless audiences with a constant supply of eclectic sonic euphoria, delivered with an unfaltering eye on big hooks and infectious grooves. Boo Seeka puts their soul-inspired, electronica-fueled trademark into every live performance, selling out rooms around the world.

What began as the solo project of Australian electropop singer-songwriter Ben "Boo" Gumbleton has grown into a project whose live credits includes top festival venues like Splendour In The Grass & Red Rocks as well as numerous sold-out headline tours across Australia, North America and Europe. "Dream" is the latest release from this master of layered synthesizers and percussive beats, which injects a luscious and futuristic vocal mix into the signature alchemy of a pulsating kick drum and sizzling live energy. With lyrics that explore the infinite possibilities of living in the moment, Boo Seeka delivers another dance-friendly anthem that inhabits the intersection between dreams and desire.
